About Us

Guian's Hotel is your perfect escape from the everyday. Enjoy luxury rooms, exquisite dining, and unforgettable hospitality in the heart of the city.

Guest Services

We offer 24/7 front desk, room service, and concierge support.

Accommodation

Our hotel offers a variety of room types including Deluxe, Double, Single, and VIP suites. Each room is equipped with modern amenities, plush bedding, free Wi-Fi, smart TV, and complimentary toiletries. Whether you're on business or vacation, comfort is guaranteed.

Facilities & Amenities

Relax in our rooftop infinity pool, unwind at the full-service spa, or stay active in our state-of-the-art fitness center. We also offer conference rooms, business center access, and complimentary high-speed internet throughout the hotel.

Guest Services

From airport transfers to in-room dining, our guest services team is available around the clock. We offer daily housekeeping, luggage storage, wake-up calls, travel assistance, and personalized concierge services to make your stay seamless.
